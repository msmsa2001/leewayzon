About Us

Leewayzon is a Mumbai-based recruitment agency offering tailored Placement Services for both Indian and international companies. Our name reflects our ethos – 'Leeway' for freedom and flexibility in hiring, and 'Zon' for our expert team operating harmoniously. With extensive industry experience and a diverse team, we excel in sourcing exceptional talent, alleviating the struggle many companies face. Our commitment to utilizing cutting-edge HR strategies, advanced technology, and a seasoned team ensures seamless connections between exceptional talent and companies poised for greatness.

What We Do

At Leewayzon, we excel in contract staffing and direct placement services. Backed by seasoned professionals, we meticulously source and manage highly skilled employees for every role. Our commitment to precision and client satisfaction ensures tailored recruitment approaches that meet unique organizational needs. Whether it's short-term contracts or permanent placements, we prioritize delivering exceptional candidates who contribute to our clients' success. Partner with us and experience the difference of expertly curated staffing solutions at Leewayzon.

Our Approach

We prioritize understanding clients' needs, delivering tailored staffing solutions that exceed expectations. Our commitment to excellence means providing candidates who not only meet job specifications but also possess the right skills, attitude, and aptitude for success. With a decade of expertise, we specialize in connecting organizations with top-tier talent, ensuring perfect alignment of skills and culture. By prioritizing the needs of clients and candidates, we cultivate long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual success. Through personalized service and strategic insights, we empower businesses to achieve their goals.

Why Choose Us?

With five years of experience, a nationwide reach, and personalized service, we offer a wealth of industry knowledge, access to diverse talent pools, and attentive approaches to staffing needs. Our dedicated team takes the time to understand your unique requirements and preferences, ensuring a personalized and attentive approach to every staffing need. Extensive industry experience, unwavering dedication to excellence, and a diverse team of experts stand poised to deliver meaningful outcomes.
