What does the term 'RPO' signify?

Recruitment refers to the process of identifying, attracting, and hiring qualified candidates for job vacancies within an organization. The goal of recruitment is to find the most suitable individuals who possess the necessary skills, qualifications, and experience to meet the requirements of the job and contribute effectively to the organisation's success.
