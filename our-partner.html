Strategic partnerships

Forging strategic alliances to combine specialized knowledge, consolidate resources, and expedite the pace of innovation

Snowflake

Snowflake is a cloud-based platform that enables data-driven businesses to mobilize data, apps, and AI across various workloads and use cases. Synechron is a Snowflake Select services partner

Databricks

Databricks is a unified, open analytics platform for building, deploying, sharing, and maintaining enterprise-grade data, analytics, and AI solutions. Synechron partners with Databricks to implement and scale data engineering, data science, full-lifecycle machine learning and business analytics initiatives

Neo4j

Neo4j is a graph database platform that enables you to store, query, analyze, and visualize data with graph-native features and vector search. Synechron is a preferred Neo4j solutions partner

Denodo

The Denodo Platform, powered by data virtualization, is a logical data integration, data management, and data delivery solution that provides a centralized data access layer that enables all users to find, query, integrate, and securely share datasets in real time. Synechron partners with Denodo to integrate the Denodo Platform within our customers’ infrastructures, increasing the value and capabilities of our customers’ solutions

DQ Labs

DQLabs is an automated modern data quality platform that delivers reliable and accurate data for better business outcomes. Synechron partners with DQ Labs to enable customers to turn data into action faster, easier, and more collaboratively

Quantexa

Quantexa is a platform that unifies data and drives AI-enabled decisions for data management, customer intelligence, risk management, fraud and security, and AML. Synechron is a Quantexa delivery partner

Solidatus

Solidatus is a data lineage platform that provides insight into complex data ecosystems with an intuitive visual interface. Synechron partners with Solidatus to deliver leading data management, governance, and intelligence solutions.
