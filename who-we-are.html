About Us

Leewayzon is a Mumbai-based recruitment agency offering tailored Placement Services for both Indian and international companies. Our name reflects our ethos – 'Leeway' for freedom and flexibility in hiring, and 'Zon' for our expert team operating harmoniously. With extensive industry experience and a diverse team, we excel in sourcing exceptional talent, alleviating the struggle many companies face. Our commitment to utilizing cutting-edge HR strategies, advanced technology, and a seasoned team ensures seamless connections between exceptional talent and companies poised for greatness.

Our company four services

I am pleased to introduce our range of services, which are designed to support both students and educational institutions in various ways:

Students Exchange Programme cum Industrial Visit

Our Students Exchange Programme cum Industrial Visit offers year-long exchanges and full college programs, including diplomas, undergraduate, and graduate studies at accredited institutions both domestically and internationally. We arrange study tours where students can study at another institution and visit industries to broaden their horizons, learn about new cultures, and gain exposure to real-world industrial processes, technologies, and operations.

Internship Opportunities

We offer internships for undergraduate and postgraduate students, allowing them to explore their passions and interests through real-world experience in their chosen fields. We have established partnerships with renowned companies like YouTube, Amazon, and more, ensuring high-quality internship placements both domestically and internationally. We charge per students 5000 Rs for an internship registration. We charge only from the students. We don't charge from college nor from any company.

Faculty Exchange Programme

Our Faculty Exchange Programme facilitates interaction and bonding among institutions through agreements with various domestic and international universities. This unique opportunity prepares educators to shape young people into positive agents of change, with a program duration of five days of interaction at another university.

Workshops and Training

We conduct workshops and training sessions in IT, HR, Communication Skills, Personality Development, and Soft Skills for corporate and institutional sectors. Our team of talented freelance trainers is dedicated to enhancing the skills of employees and students.
