What is Data Analysis?

Data analysis is the process of inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and modeling data to uncover useful information, draw conclusions, and support decision-making. It involves examining datasets to identify patterns, trends, correlations, and insights that can inform business strategies, operations, and outcomes. Data analysis encompasses a wide range of techniques, methodologies, and tools, and it is commonly used across various industries and disciplines to extract actionable insights from data.
