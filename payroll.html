Types of Pay-roll Service

  • In-house Pay-roll

In-house payroll refers to managing payroll processes internally within the organisation. The company handles all aspects of payroll, including salary calculations, deductions, taxes, and payment processing, using its own resources and systems.

  • Permanent Payroll

Permanent payroll involves hiring employees directly as full-time or part-time staff members of the organisation. The company manages payroll internally for these permanent employees, providing regular salaries, benefits, and complying with labour laws and regulations.

  • Contract Staffing or Temporary Pay-roll

IT companies often hire contract or temporary staff for specific projects or periods. In such cases, contract staffing agencies may manage payroll for these temporary employees, handling salary payments, benefits administration, and compliance with labour regulations.

  • Third-Party Pay-roll

Third-party payroll services are provided by external service providers specialising in payroll management. These providers handle payroll processes on behalf of the client company, offering services such as salary calculations, tax filings, compliance management, and payment processing.

  • Pay-roll Processing Software Solutions

Payroll processing software solutions are tools that facilitate in-house payroll management. These software applications automate payroll processes, including salary calculations, tax deductions, time tracking, and generating payroll reports, offering efficiency and accuracy in payroll administration.

  • Global Pay-roll Management

Global payroll management services cater to IT companies with an international presence. These services help manage payroll processes across multiple countries, ensuring compliance with local tax laws, labour regulations, and currency conversions for diverse workforce locations.

  • Compliance and Regulatory Support

Some payroll service providers specialise in offering compliance and regulatory support, helping IT companies navigate complex payroll regulations, tax laws, and labour requirements, ensuring adherence to legal standards and mitigating compliance risks.

  • Offshore Payroll Services

Offshore payroll services involve outsourcing payroll functions to a service provider located in another country. IT companies may choose offshore payroll services to benefit from cost savings, access to specialised expertise, or to streamline global payroll management for their international workforce.
